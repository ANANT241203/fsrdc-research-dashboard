Project Summary

This interactive dashboard presents insights from over 3,000 research outputs associated with U.S. Census FSRDCs (Federal Statistical Research Data Centers). The visualizations show trends in publication activity, identify prolific RDCs, and highlight leading authors contributing to the FSRDC research ecosystem.
